Chapter Two — The Wordmark

A custom hand-cut display lettering.

The TANDOORI NIGHTS wordmark is a tribal-cut display face — jagged terminals, irregular cap heights, almost like cloth torn against a hot tandoor. Use it large; never letter-spaced; never modified.

01 · Primary on Red
02 · Red on Cream
03 · Saffron on Olive
04 · Red on Turmeric
05 · Saffron on Black

Clear Space

Always preserve clear space equal to the cap height of the "T" on every side. Never crowd the mark with type, photography, or other graphics.

Minimum Size

Digital — 120px wide. Print — 1.25 inches wide. Below this size, switch to the simplified "TN" monogram (in development).

Don't

Don't outline, drop-shadow (except subtle on photo), recolor outside this system, condense, italicize, or rebuild in another typeface. The lettering is custom.
