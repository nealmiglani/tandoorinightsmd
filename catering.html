Catering & Private Events.

Two decades of weddings, anniversaries, board dinners, and Diwalis — across Montgomery County and the greater DC area.

We have catered nearly two thousand events. The dishes leave our kitchen the same temperature, the same minute, the same standard as the ones we plate in the dining room. That is the only way we know how to do it.

What We Cater

From a tray of samosas to a 500-person reception.

Weddings

Mehendi, sangeet, baraat, and reception — full or partial coordination across the weekend. Live tandoor stations, dosa stations, chaat counters, and our signature seven-course tasting menu for the head table.

Corporate

Lunch trays, hot buffets, plated dinners, and AV-ready private rooms. We have catered for the NIH, NIST, IBM, Lockheed, the Universities at Shady Grove, and dozens of mid-sized firms across the corridor.

Family Celebrations

Diwali, Holi, Eid, anniversaries, milestone birthdays. Drop-off, full-service, or in our private dining room. We help you plan around the season.

Service Areas

Gaithersburg, Rockville, Germantown, Bethesda, Potomac, North Potomac, Frederick, Silver Spring, Arlington, McLean, and Washington DC. Travel fees apply outside of Montgomery County.
