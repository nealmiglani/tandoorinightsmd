Getting Here

By car, by foot, by neighbor.

Kentlands is a walkable village built around Market Square — brick sidewalks, tree-lined commons, and a dozen of the best restaurants in Montgomery County in a six-block radius.

By Car · Free Parking

From I-270, take Exit 11 onto Quince Orchard Road North (MD-124). Continue half a mile to Kentlands Boulevard and turn left into the village. Market Street is one block in. Free parking on Market Street and throughout Market Square — surface lots, on-street, and the rear lot off Tschiffely Square.

From Shady Grove Metro

Twelve minutes by car from the Shady Grove Metro (Red Line). Ride On bus 74 connects Shady Grove Station to Kentlands. Rideshare drop-off is on Market Street, directly in front of our door.

From the Neighborhoods

Walking distance from anywhere in Kentlands and Lakelands · 6 min from Crown · 14 from Rockville Town Center · 18 from Bethesda Row · 22 from Potomac · 28 from Frederick · 35 from Dupont Circle. Plenty close to make a Tuesday-night reason.

Accessibility

Wheelchair accessible entrance and dining room. Accessible restrooms. ADA-compliant parking spaces on Market Street. Service animals welcome.

Before You Arrive

A few small notes.

Do I need a reservation?

Recommended Friday and Saturday evenings, especially after 7:00pm. Walk-ins are always welcome — the bar seats six and is a lovely place to wait.

Large parties?

For parties of six or more, please call 301-947-4007. Our private dining room seats up to 24 and books quickly during the holidays.

Dress code?

Smart casual. We are a fine dining room, but jackets are not required. We ask that guests refrain from athletic wear at dinner service.

Children?

Welcome at all hours. We have a children's menu and high chairs available — just let us know when you book.

Dietary restrictions?

Half our menu is vegetarian. We have extensive vegan-friendly and gluten-free options, and we accommodate Jain dietary requirements with notice. All meat in our kitchen is Halal-certified.

Gift cards?

Available year-round through our online portal — physical and digital, in any denomination. Purchase here.

We'll see you tonight.

Reservations recommended for weekend evenings. Walk-ins always welcome.
